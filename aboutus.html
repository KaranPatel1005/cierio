1 - Research & Discovery

Overview:

This is the most important aspect of our whole design process, where we develop a holistic overview of our client's brand, goals, industry, and competitors. We work closely with the client to gain an in-depth understanding of their company in order to deliver a custom solution that works.

Objectives:

  • Acquire an in-depth understanding of clients products and/or services
  • Understand the client's target audience and reach
  • Research the competition present within the industry to see what works and what doesn't
  • Understand the clients present and future goals
  • Document client's design preferences in terms of color, typography, message, etc.

Deliverables:

  • Logo design
  • Brand guidelines document
  • Website sitemap

Timeline:

This process generally takes 2-3 weeks to complete

2 - Design Stage

Overview:

Moving through our iterative design process, we provide our clients a high-fidelity wireframe design of their new website. The purpose of this phase is to provide a pixel-perfect look and feel of the website before a single line of code is written to eradicate any surprises during development.

Objectives:

  • Design website based on website sitemap created
  • Design all pages and elements with UI/UX principles in mind to maximize conversion
  • Design desktop and mobile versions of the website
  • Stay consistent with new or existing brand guidelines
  • Acquire all needed copy and assets for the website

Deliverables:

  • Desktop & mobile high-fidelity wireframes of the entire website.
  • A working prototype of all pages if agreed upon

Timeline:

This process generally takes 2-3 weeks to complete

3 - Development

Overview:

In the development stage, we start to develop the client's new website based on the approved wireframe design. The website is optimized for technical SEO from day one by being fully responsive, fast, and containing proper internal linking and naming structures.

Objectives:

  • Develop each page with pixel-perfect accuracy to approved wireframe design
  • Meet all functionality requirements agreed upon
  • Complete proper testing of website and internal features
  • Ensure the website is properly optimized from a technical SEO standpoint

Deliverables:

  • Access to view website on Cierio in-house server.

Timeline:

This process generally takes 3-5 weeks

4 - Deployment & Analytics

Overview:

Upon completion of development the final website is migrated to the clients hosting environment. Upon deployment user analytics tracking is set up to provide data for future marketing campaigns.

Objectives:

  • Migrate & deploy completed website to clients environment
  • Configure google analytics
  • Configure facebook pixel

Deliverables:

  • Deployed Website
  • Google Analytics tracking
  • Facebook pixel tracking

Timeline:

This process generally takes 1 week

Unlike many other design agencies we do not use templates and build all of our websites from scratch with a custom design tailored to your business. Our website design services should not be viewed as just another business expense but an investment and sales tool that should earn you money in the long run.

With that being said, the majority of our sites are between the $3,000 - $10,000 price range but can be more or less depending on the exact project requirements. We will ask questions, assess your needs, and provide a proposal with a quote based on our assessment.

We split all of our projects into two simple payments, each totaling 50% of the quoted project price. The first payment is to be paid prior to the start of the project and is non-refundable. The second payment is to be paid prior to us migrating your website to your hosting server. It is very likely that your website will not be fully complete (form setup, user-analytics tracking, domain-specific functionalities, etc..) upon migration. However, in order to migrate and complete the last tasks we must receive full project payment. If any additional costs were incurred over the duration of the project they will be paid with the second (50% of original quoted price) payment.
